Department of Computer Science & Engineering (DST-FIST Sponsored Department)


M.Tech CNS

Rules & Regulations Curriculum

Program Educational Objectives
  • To provide advanced knowledge related to networking and securing wired, wireless, and mobile, adhoc wireless and sensor networks.
  • To provide knowledge related to designing, developing huge networks through usage of Network simulators.
  • To make the students use security related infrastructure and its related implementation through usage of one of the market recognized solutions.
  • To provide the background knowledge required to undertake research in the fields of networking and securing the networks and find solutions to the complex problems.
  • To make the students learn lifelong for making them up-to-date with all the advancing technologies
  • To make the students work individually and in teams especially in executing the projects in the fields of networking and security
  • To make the students understand the norms and standards related to field of networking and security so as to preserve professional ethics in the filed
  • To make the students understand various Society related issues and include them as functional requirements of different projects that are related to networking and securing the networks
  • To describe, design, develop and implement various applications in the field of networking and security

Programm Outcomes
  • Must be able to apply all the concepts related to networking and security in specifying , analyzing, designing, developing and troubleshooting various types of networks
  • Must be able to evaluate the performance of networks of different sizes
  • Must be able to prototype different types and sizes of networks using networking simulators
  • Must be able to implement various security related infrastructure.
  • Must be able to undertake projects that implement different types of networks and implementing different types of security systems
  • Must be able to trouble shoot the failures occurring in different types of networks
  • Must be able to make extension to networking and security related protocols and develop an environment to check the working of the same.
