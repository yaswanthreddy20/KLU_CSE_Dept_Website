Rules & Regulations Areas of Specializations


Objectives of the Academic research programs offered by Computer Science and Engineering Department

1.To offer research programs in the specializations that include

  • Computer Networks and security

  • Software Engineering

  • Knowledge Engineering

  • High Performance Computing

  • Cloud Computing

  • Network Security and Digital forensics

  • Soft Computing

2.To conduct research in the emerging areas that include

  • Wireless Sensor Networks

  • Wireless Channel Simulation

  • Wireless Network Resource Optimizers

  • Remote sensors

  • Quantum Key Cryptography

  • Performance analysis of network resources

  • Encryption & Broadcasting

  • Secured systems and attack patterns

  • Multi cast data transfer in Mobile networks

  • Cryptography conventions

  • Adaption of Cleanroom software engineering to different classes of applications

  • Extreme programming

  • Aspect oriented programming

  • Distributed Data Mining

  • Cognitive Data Mining

  • Spatial temporal data mining

  • Cognition built knowledge discovery

  • Word sense extraction

  • Spatial temporal data mining

  • High Performance Computing

  • Virtual computing

  1. To offer extramural research programs to the Industry persons

  2. To conduct Joint Research with foreign Professors in the fields of speciation

  3. To develop excellence center in the field of Computer Networks and security

  4. To move research into class rooms through examples, courses, assignments and projects

  5. To upgrade and update all the research labs related to CSE with the tools and the equipment required for conducting the research in specialization and emerging areas

  6. To establish High performance computing lab

  7. To recognize the research requirements of the neighborhood and conduct research in the required areas

  8. To make the students participate in the research through term papers, B. Tech projects, M. Tech desertions and PhD thesis.

  9. To make the students to be innovative through mini projects, B. Tech projects, M. Tech desertions and PhD thesis.

  10. To conduct one international Conference per year so as to align with International research community

  11. To facilitate post-doctoral research in the chosen fields of study

  12. To organize professional bodies and conduct professional events

  13. To offer research programs in the inter-discipline and Trans-discipline areas

Expected Academic research outcomes

  1. Research publications in the reputed Journals having impact factor more than 1.0

  2. At least one patent per specialization stream

  3. At least 5 PhD qualifiers per year

  4. Development of a real life applications using the findings of every research program

  5. To develop a working model out of every research which is oriented towards real life application
